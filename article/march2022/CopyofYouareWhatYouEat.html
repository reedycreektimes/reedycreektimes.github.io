You Are What You Eat

https://draxe.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/04/EssentialNutrients_FB.jpg

Have you ever heard the phrase “An apple a day keeps the doctor away”, or “You are what you eat”. What if I told you that they were true? Well, not exactly. What they are saying is that you should keep a healthy diet. “But why”, you might say. “My diet is just fine”. Is it?

Let’s start on the definition of a healthy diet. A healthy diet is a diet that maintains or improves your body’s overall condition. In other words, a healthy diet is a diet that helps your body’s current state. What exactly do you need for a healthy diet. Your body needs proteins, vitamins, minerals, fats, sodium, and natural sugars. These are essential nutrients that your body needs to keep functioning correctly. But, there’s a catch. You need to keep a balance. For example, too much protein will be stored as excess fat, or too many carbs can lead to diabetes or other blood related problems. So, exactly what should I eat?

Try to go towards foods that are rich in all of these essential nutrients. Eat at least two servings of fruit and vegetables per day, but don’t over do it. Too much of something is still bad for you, including fruits and vegetables. Also, too much meat is bad, especially if it’s processed. Processed and red meat can increase your risks of having diabetes and coronary cancer by at least 2%. It might not seem like a lot, but it can happen. Don’t not eat meat, but try to bring other foods in your diet than just meat. Meat is an excellent source of protein, but protein can also be found in eggs, lentils, and soy. Also, most of the carbohydrates found in the food we eat are really bad for you. These “bad” carbs can be found in pretzels, chips, popcorn, doughnuts, french fries, processed cereal, and canned fruit. Instead, eat those foods only once in a while, and stick to healthier alternatives, like seeds, nuts, fruit, legumes, whole grains, and tubers. Too many carbs are bad, but too little carbs are bad for you as well. Sugar and sodium can also be bad for you in too much or too little quantities, and it's better to try and avoid processed sugars or too much salt in your food.

You might be thinking, “What does this do for me?” It can keep you strong and healthy, and provide all the energy you need for a day. It also can reduce your chance for cancer and other illnesses. A healthy diet can provide for your body and your mind. A healthy diet is very vital for your health.
